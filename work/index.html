Kingdom:Legendary War


Kingdom: Legendary War (Korean: 킹덤: 레전더리 워; RR: Kingdeom: Rejeondeori Wo) is a television program that currently airs on Mnet beginning April 1, 2021, on every Thursday at 19:50 (KST). It is known as the male counterpart of Queendom, and the sequel of Road to Kingdom.

The program is a battle behind six trending K-pop boy groups: BtoB, IKon, SF9, The Boyz, Stray Kids, and ATEEZ. The program features 4 rounds of performances, with the final round being broadcast live.

On January 22, 2021, Mnet confirmed that the hosts of the show will be TVXQ.

The winner of the show will get a reality show specially for the winning boy group, plus a "Kingdom Week" special show.

The Contestants



BTOB

BTOB (비투비) currently consists of 6 members: Eunkwang, Minhyuk, Changsub, Hyunsik, Peniel and Sungjae. Ilhoon left the group on December 31, 2020. They debuted on March 21, 2012, under CUBE Entertainment.

IKON

iKON (아이콘) currently consists of 6 members: Bobby, Jay, Ju-ne, Song, DK and Chan. B.I left the group on June 12th, 2019. iKON was created from the survival show Mix & Match. iKON debuted on September 15th, 2015 under YG Entertainment.

SF9

SF9 (에스에프나인) consists of 9 members: Youngbin, Inseong, Jaeyoon, Dawon, Zuho, Rowoon, Yoo Taeyang, Hwiyoung and Chani. The band debuted on October 5, 2016, under FNC Entertainment.

THE BOYZ

The Boyz (더보이즈) is a 11-member South Korean boy group under Cre.Ker Entertainment. The group consists of Sangyeon, Jacob, Younghoon, Hyunjae, Juyeon, Kevin, New, Q, Juhaknyeon, Sunwoo, and Eric. The group debuted on December 6, 2017. Originally a 12-piece group, former member Hwall officially left the group in October 2019 due to health reasons.

more

STRAY KIDS

Stray Kids (스트레이 키즈) is a South Korean boy group under JYP Entertainment. The group currently consists of Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. Woojin left the group on October 27th, 2019. Stray Kids was created through the survival program with the same name, Stray Kids. Stray Kids debuted on March 25, 2018.

more

ATEEZ

ATEEZ (에이티즈) is an 8-member group under KQ Entertainment. The group consists of: Hongjoong, Seonghwa, Yunho, Yeosang, San, Mingi, Wooyoung, and Jongho. Junyoung will no longer be apart of the final line up. They debuted on October 24th, 2018 with the songs ‘Treasure’ and ‘Pirate King’. On July 8th 2019, ATEEZ signed with USA record label RCA Records.

The Rules

The overall final ranking of the show is based on the following criteria:

1 Self-evaluation points 25%
2 Experts' voting 25%
3 Global voting 40%
4 Performance video view count 10%
